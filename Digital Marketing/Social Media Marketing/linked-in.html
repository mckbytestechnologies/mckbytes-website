Common questions

What teams typically want to know.

Eight quick answers. Have something specific in mind? We'll respond within one business day.

01

How much ad spend do I need to make LinkedIn worth it?

For paid campaigns we recommend a minimum of ₹2.5L/mo (~$3K). Below that, audience pools are too tight and learning phases take too long. Organic-first engagements start much lower.

02

How long before we see meetings booked?

Most accounts see the first qualified meetings within 30–45 days. ABM programs typically take 6–8 weeks to fully prime, then run consistently from there.

03

Do you write the thought leadership content?

Yes. Our senior B2B writers ghost-write founder & executive posts based on long-form interviews. You approve every piece before it ships — your voice, our craft.

04

Who owns the ad account & data?

You always own your LinkedIn Business Manager, Insight Tag, and every creative asset. We work inside your account so you can pause or switch us out without losing a single data point.

05

Do you integrate with our CRM?

Yes — HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive, and most modern CRMs. Lead Gen Forms sync directly so your sales team gets fresh leads in their pipeline within minutes.

06

What does reporting look like?

A live dashboard updated daily, a weekly summary email, and a deep monthly review with your senior strategist. Every metric mapped to pipeline and revenue.

07

Do you handle Employee Advocacy too?

Yes — we set up the program, build the content library, train your team, and run the incentive structure that keeps advocacy alive past month two.

08

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes — 30 days notice. No annual lock-ins, no surprise fees. If we're not delivering, you shouldn't be paying us. We earn next month's retainer every month.
