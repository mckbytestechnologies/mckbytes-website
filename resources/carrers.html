How we work

Four values that shape everything.

We're not a factory. We're a studio — and studios have standards. These four principles guide every project, every hire, every decision.

01

Craft first

We hold work to a standard most agencies skip. Quality isn't a checklist — it's a mindset that shows in every pixel, every line of code, every campaign brief.

02

Radical ownership

Every team member owns their domain end-to-end. We don't pass problems around — we solve them, communicate proactively, and iterate relentlessly.

03

Bias for speed

We ship, learn, and iterate — in that order. Decisions made with 70% of the information, executed fast and refined in production, outperform plans perfected in meetings.

04

Grow or stagnate

The industry moves fast. We invest in learning — talks, budget, tools, feedback loops. If you aren't growing, you're falling behind. We choose growth.

The process

How we hire — honestly.

Four steps. No trick questions, no pointless rounds. We respect your time and move fast.

01

Application

Email your resume and a short note (why MCKBytes, what you bring). Portfolio welcome for creative roles.

02

Intro Call

30-minute conversation with a lead. We discuss your experience, interests, and answer every question you have about us.

03

Skills Task

A focused, paid task (2–4 hours). Real work from our stack so you know what you're signing up for. We review it in 48 hrs.

04

Offer & Onboard

Offer letter within 5 days. Onboarding is structured, mentored, and designed to get you shipping work in week one.
