FAQ

Choosing a Logo Design Service Company

Everything you need to know before investing in professional logo design.

What should I look for in a logo design company?

Look for experience, a strong portfolio, client reviews, clear pricing, and a collaborative design process. The right agency will take time to understand your brand before designing anything.

How much does professional logo design typically cost?

Professional logo design varies based on complexity, the agency's reputation, and the level of customisation. McKbytes offers packages for every budget — from startups to enterprise rebrands. Contact us for a personalised quote.

Should I choose a freelancer or a logo design agency?

An agency provides a structured process, team-based review, accountability, and long-term support. Freelancers can be cost-effective but may lack the process rigor and backup that a team provides for critical brand work.

How long does it take to design a logo?

A professional logo typically takes 1–3 weeks depending on complexity, the number of revisions, and client feedback turnaround. Rush projects can be accommodated — discuss your timeline with our team.

What file formats should I receive for my logo?

You should receive your logo in vector formats (SVG, AI, EPS) for scalability, and raster formats (PNG, JPG) for digital use. McKbytes delivers a complete file package including transparent background variants and multiple colour versions.

Will I get full ownership of my logo after it's completed?

Yes. McKbytes transfers full intellectual property rights to you upon final payment. Your logo is yours — exclusively, completely, and permanently.

Can I request revisions if I don't like the initial concepts?

Absolutely. Our design process includes structured revision rounds. We collaborate closely with you until the final design fully aligns with your vision and brand standards.

What is a logo design brief and why is it important?

A design brief is a document that captures your brand vision, audience, competitors, color preferences, and style references. It serves as the foundation for every design decision and dramatically improves the quality and relevance of the final output.

Will the logo be scalable for all media and sizes?

Yes. All McKbytes logos are designed in vector format, ensuring perfect clarity at any scale — from a business card to a billboard, app icon to social media profile.

What's the difference between a logo and a brand identity?

A logo is the visual mark of your brand. Brand identity is the broader system — including color palette, typography, imagery guidelines, and usage rules. McKbytes offers both logo design and full brand identity packages.

What's the risk of using a cheap logo design service?

Cheap services often use templates, deliver low-resolution files, transfer no IP rights, and produce designs that lack originality or strategic thinking. The cost of rebranding later is far higher than investing in quality from the start.

Should I consider local or international logo designers?

Both can produce excellent results. McKbytes serves clients globally — remote collaboration is seamless with our structured process, clear communication, and dedicated project management from brief to final delivery.
