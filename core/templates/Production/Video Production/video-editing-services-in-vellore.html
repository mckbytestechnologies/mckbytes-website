How we work

Five phases. One seamless editing process.

From raw footage to final export, we own every stage of post-production so you get a polished video without the hassle.

01
INGEST · DAY 1–2

Organizing your footage.

We receive your raw footage, organize by scene and take, sync audio, and create a project structure — setting up everything for efficient editing.

  • Footage ingestion & backup
  • Media organization & logging
  • Audio sync & multi-cam grouping
  • Project setup & timeline creation
02
ROUGH CUT · DAY 3–5

Building the narrative.

First assembly of selected footage — establishing story structure, pacing, and flow. You review the rough cut and provide feedback.

  • Selecting best takes
  • Sequence assembly & narrative flow
  • Pacing & rhythm development
  • Rough cut review & feedback
03
FINE CUT · WEEK 2

Refining every frame.

Incorporating feedback, fine-tuning edits, adding transitions, and polishing the sequence — delivering a locked picture cut.

  • Incorporate feedback & revisions
  • Precision trimming & timing
  • Transition & effect polish
  • Picture lock & approval
04
FINISH · WEEK 3

Adding polish and depth.

Color grading, sound design, motion graphics, and visual effects — transforming the fine cut into a finished masterpiece.

  • Color grading & correction
  • Sound design & audio mixing
  • Motion graphics & VFX
  • Final review & approval
05
EXPORT · WEEK 3–4

Delivering everywhere.

Master export, social cut-downs, captions, and delivery in every format your channels need — plus secure file transfer.

  • Master file export (HD/4K)
  • Social media cut-downs
  • Caption & subtitle files
  • Secure delivery & archiving
