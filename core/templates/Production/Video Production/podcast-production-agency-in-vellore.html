How we work

Five phases. One end-to-end podcast production process.

From concept through distribution, we own every stage of podcast production so your team focuses on great conversations.

01
CONCEPT · WEEK 1

Defining your podcast vision.

Deep-dive strategy sessions to define show concept, target audience, format, episode structure, and success metrics — building a podcast that serves your goals.

  • Show concept & format definition
  • Target audience & persona development
  • Episode structure & segment planning
  • Branding & artwork design
  • Intro/outro music composition
02
PRE-PRODUCTION · WEEK 2–3

Planning every episode.

Guest booking, episode scripting, research, and production scheduling — ensuring every episode is set up for success before recording begins.

  • Guest identification & outreach
  • Episode research & briefing
  • Question script development
  • Recording schedule coordination
  • Technical preparation & testing
03
RECORDING · ON-AIR

Capturing great conversations.

Professional recording sessions — in-studio or remote — with broadcast-quality audio capture, real-time monitoring, and host direction.

  • Professional audio capture
  • Remote recording coordination
  • Real-time audio monitoring
  • Host & guest direction
  • Backup recording redundancy
04
POST-PRODUCTION · 3–5 DAYS

Crafting the final episode.

Editing, mixing, mastering, and adding intro/outro, ads, and music — delivering a polished, professional episode ready for distribution.

  • Audio cleaning & noise reduction
  • Removing filler words & pauses
  • Leveling, EQ & compression
  • Intro/outro & music integration
  • Mastering to broadcast standards
05
DISTRIBUTION · GO-LIVE

Sharing with the world.

Show notes, episode descriptions, transcriptions, and multi-platform distribution — plus promotion assets for social media.

  • Show notes & episode description
  • Full episode transcription
  • Apple/Spotify/Google submission
  • Social media promotional assets
  • Analytics dashboard setup
