How we work

Five phases. One seamless animation process.

From concept through final render, we own every stage of animation production so your vision comes to life exactly as imagined.

01
CONCEPT · WEEK 1

Defining your vision.

Deep discovery sessions to understand your story, audience, and visual goals — then concept development, style exploration, and scriptwriting that captures your essence.

  • Story & vision discovery
  • Style exploration & references
  • Scriptwriting & story development
  • Concept & mood board approval
02
DESIGN · WEEK 2–3

Building your world.

Character designs, environment art, color palettes, storyboarding, and animatics — every visual element designed and approved before animation begins.

  • Character & environment design
  • Storyboarding & scene planning
  • Animatic & timing development
  • Design approval
03
ANIMATION · WEEK 4–6

Making it move.

Keyframe animation, character rigging, motion graphics, and scene transitions — bringing your storyboard to life frame by frame.

  • Keyframe animation production
  • Character rigging & movement
  • Scene transitions & effects
  • Rough animation review
04
LIGHTING & TEXTURING · WEEK 6–7

Adding depth and realism.

For 3D projects — texturing, lighting, shading, and camera work that adds cinematic quality and visual depth to every scene.

  • 3D texturing & materials
  • Lighting & shadow setup
  • Camera animation & composition
  • Visual polish & refinement
05
RENDER & DELIVERY · WEEK 7–8

Bringing it to life.

Final rendering, compositing, sound design, music, and mastering — delivering a polished, professional animation ready for your audience.

  • Final render & compositing
  • Sound design & music composition
  • Color grading & final polish
  • Multi-format export & delivery

Quick answers about animation services.

2D animation has a flat, illustrative look — like classic cartoons or motion graphics. It's great for explainers, brand stories, and character-driven content. 3D animation has depth, texture, and realistic lighting — ideal for product visualizations, architectural walkthroughs, and cinematic storytelling. We'll recommend the best style for your project.

A 60-90 second 2D animated video typically takes 6-8 weeks. 3D animation takes 8-12 weeks due to modeling, texturing, lighting, and rendering time. Longer-form content or complex scenes may take additional time. We provide detailed timelines upfront.
