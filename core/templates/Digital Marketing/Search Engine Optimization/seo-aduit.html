Questions we get asked most often about our SEO audit services.

What is an SEO audit and why do I need one?

An SEO audit is a comprehensive analysis of your website's ability to rank in search engines. It identifies technical issues, content gaps, and competitive opportunities. You need one because most websites have hidden problems that silently kill rankings — and you can't fix what you can't see.

How long does an SEO audit take?

Our standard SEO audit takes 7-10 business days from kickoff to delivery. This includes full technical crawl, manual review, competitor analysis, and report compilation. Rush audits available in 3-5 days.

Do you provide a free SEO audit?

We offer a complimentary 10-point website scan as a starting point — covering basic technical health, page speed, and mobile usability. A full comprehensive audit is our paid service, delivered with complete documentation and strategic recommendations.

What tools do you use for SEO audits?

We use enterprise-grade tools including Screaming Frog, SEMrush, Ahrefs, Google Search Console, Google Analytics, Core Web Vitals, Lighthouse, and proprietary crawlers — combined with manual expert analysis.

Will you fix the issues you find?

Yes. Our audit includes a prioritized action plan your team can execute. Or we can implement the fixes for you — technical SEO fixes, content updates, and migration support — as a separate engagement.

How often should I get an SEO audit?

At minimum annually. Recommended: quarterly for competitive industries, after any major site update, or before any significant SEO investment. Regular audits catch issues before they impact rankings.

Do you audit local SEO as well?

Absolutely. Our local SEO audit covers Google Business Profile health, local citation consistency, review signals, local keyword targeting, and map-pack optimization opportunities.

What's the difference between an SEO audit and an SEO strategy?

An audit diagnoses current issues and opportunities. A strategy builds the roadmap to achieve specific goals. Most clients start with an audit, then engage us for ongoing strategy and execution.

Do you audit e-commerce websites?

Yes — we specialize in e-commerce SEO audits covering product schema, faceted navigation, duplicate product pages, category structure, internal linking, and shopping feed optimization.

Can you audit a site before we buy it?

Yes — pre-acquisition SEO due diligence is a common use case. We identify hidden technical debt, ranking risks, and backlink quality issues before you commit to purchase.
