Questions we get asked most often about our local SEO services.

What is local SEO and how is it different from regular SEO?

Local SEO focuses on optimizing your online presence for location-based searches — "near me", "[service] in [city]", and map pack results. Regular SEO targets broader, non-location-specific queries. Local SEO prioritizes Google Business Profile, local citations, and review signals over traditional backlinks and content scale.

How long does local SEO take to show results?

Local SEO typically shows initial movement in 30-60 days — citation fixes, GBP optimization, and review generation can impact map pack rankings quickly. Dominating competitive local markets usually takes 3-6 months of consistent optimization.

Do I need a physical storefront for local SEO?

No. Service-area businesses (plumbers, cleaners, consultants) without a physical storefront can still rank locally using service-area settings in GBP and localized landing pages for each suburb or neighbourhood you serve.

What's the most important local SEO ranking factor?

Google's local algorithm prioritizes three pillars: proximity (searcher's location relative to your business), prominence (reviews, citations, backlinks), and relevance (category match, service offering). Among these, review quantity + quality and GBP completeness have the strongest correlation with map pack rankings.

How many reviews do I need to rank locally?

More matters, but review velocity and recency matter more. Businesses with 50-100 recent, high-quality reviews typically outrank those with 500 older reviews. We focus on consistent, authentic review generation — not volume at any cost.

Can you help with multiple business locations?

Absolutely. We specialize in multi-location local SEO — individual GBP pages for each location, location-specific landing pages, citation management across all addresses, and consolidated reporting for enterprise clients.

Do you optimize for voice search?

Yes — voice search is inherently local. People ask their devices for "near me" and conversational queries. Our local SEO strategy includes natural language optimization, FAQ schema, and question-targeted content that aligns with how people speak.

What's included in your Google Business Profile optimization?

Complete GBP setup or audit — NAP verification, category and attribute optimization, business hours (including special hours), photo uploads and management, post scheduling, Q&A management, product/service listing, and GBP insights monitoring.

How do you track local SEO ROI?

We track map pack positions, local keyword rankings, GBP insights (direction requests, calls, website clicks), call tracking numbers, and when possible, foot traffic attribution via Google's store visits metric — all reported monthly.

What happens if my Google Business Profile gets suspended?

We have a proven reinstatement process. Common suspension reasons include address inconsistencies, service area misconfiguration, or guideline violations. We audit, fix, and submit reinstatement requests — typically resolved in 7-14 days.
