Audience strategy

Four layers of audience — sequenced for revenue.

Every audience needs a different message. We sequence creative to match exactly where someone is in their relationship with your brand — from never-heard-of-you cold prospects to ready-to-buy retargeting pools.

C

Cold prospecting

Interest-based, lookalike 1–5%, and broad targeting with strong creative hooks. The widest top of the funnel.

W

Warm engagement

Video viewers 25–75%, post engagers, profile visitors. They know you — now we deepen the relationship.

H

Hot intent

Site visitors 30d, product viewers, ATC. We close them with offers, social proof, and urgency.

B

Existing buyers

Upsell, cross-sell, LTV expansion, win-back. Your most valuable audience, treated like it.

Common questions

Everything you'd ask on a discovery call.

Still have something specific in mind? Reach out — we'll respond within one business day.

QHow long before we see results?

Most accounts see a measurable lift within 30–45 days. The first 2 weeks are pixel setup, audience research, and creative production. Weeks 3–6 are testing and identifying winners. From week 6 onward, we scale what works.

QDo you require a minimum ad spend?

Our Starter plan suits brands spending ₹50K/mo upward. Below that, the math rarely works — there's not enough data to optimize meaningfully. We're happy to recommend a different starting point if needed.

QWho owns the ad account & assets?

You always own your Meta Business Manager, ad account, pixel data, and every creative asset. We work inside your account so you can pause or replace us without losing a single data point.

QDo you handle creative production?

Yes. Our in-house studio shoots, edits, designs, and writes every ad — typically 15–20 creative variations per month, all on-brand, all platform-native. No outsourcing.

QWhat reporting do we get?

Live dashboards updated daily, weekly summary emails, and a deep monthly review call with your senior strategist. Every metric tied to revenue, not vanity.

QCan I cancel anytime?

Yes — 30 days notice. No annual contracts, no lock-ins. If we're not delivering, you shouldn't be paying us. We earn the next month's retainer every month.
