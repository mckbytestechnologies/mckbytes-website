Our point of view

Most ads fail. Here's why ours don't.

The Mckbytes Manifesto Issue No. 042 Vellore · India

Advertising has a credibility problem. Most of what gets made is forgettable — chasing trends, copying what worked last quarter, hoping nobody notices that the strategy is "post more." That's not advertising. That's noise. And noise is expensive.

"A good idea is worth more than ten executions. A great idea is worth more than a hundred."

We start every project with a question no algorithm can answer: why should anyone care? Because attention isn't earned by frequency, it's earned by relevance. By tension. By saying something the rest of the market is too scared to say.

Our team is small on purpose. Senior strategists, senior designers, senior writers — no juniors hiding behind a presentation deck. Every campaign is owned by people who've built brands, shipped films, and watched ads on hoardings they themselves designed.

If you want safe, predictable, on-brief work — you'll find plenty of agencies that deliver that. If you want work that earns the right to be seen, that builds equity over time, that your audience actually shares — that's what we make.

This is Mckbytes. We make ads people remember. Not because we're loud, but because we have something to say.

Our process

Five panels. One great campaign.

From the first brief to the final hoarding, every campaign follows the same five-step storyboard.

Panel 01 1

Brief & Discover

Sit down with you, understand the brand, the buyer, the business goal. The deeper this gets, the better the work.

Panel 02 2

Strategy & Insight

Find the tension — the thing the audience cares about but no one's saying. That's our starting line.

Panel 03 3

Concept & Craft

Three big ideas, presented in person. We sweat the craft on whichever you pick — words, visuals, motion, sound.

Panel 04 4

Production

Shoot, design, code, edit. In-house teams keep the craft tight and the timelines honest.

Panel 05 5

Launch & Learn

Live the campaign with you. Read the data, iterate the creative, scale what works. Repeat.

Common questions

Things teams usually want to know.

Have something specific in mind? Drop us a line and we'll reply within one business day.

Engagement

How do projects typically start?

With a 45-minute discovery call. We listen, ask questions, then come back with a written proposal — scope, timeline, team, and a transparent price. No fluff.

Pricing

How do you price campaigns?

Project-based for one-off campaigns, monthly retainer for ongoing work. Pricing depends on scope — we share a real number before you ever commit.

Timelines

How long does a campaign take?

A single-channel campaign: 3–4 weeks from brief to live. Integrated multi-channel campaigns: 6–10 weeks. Strategy & brand identity work: 4–8 weeks.

Ownership

Who owns the creative output?

You do — fully and forever. All creative assets, source files, copy and master files are yours from delivery onwards. We retain portfolio rights only.

Industries

Which industries do you specialise in?

D2C, hospitality, real estate, healthcare, education, fashion, F&B and B2B SaaS. If your category isn't here, ask — we may still be a great fit.

Edits

How many rounds of revisions are included?

Two rounds on every deliverable, with one round of minor tweaks at final stage. Bigger changes after that are scoped as a small change-order so timelines stay honest.
