How we work

Four phases. One end-to-end process.

From discovery through delivery, we own every stage of production so your team focuses on the brand, not the pipeline.

01
DISCOVERY · WEEK 1

Understanding your story.

In-depth research, deliberate questioning, and attentive listening to gain a profound understanding of your needs, goals, and market landscape — before we touch a camera.

  • Project scoping & goal definition
  • Stakeholder interviews & requirements gathering
  • Audience & persona development
  • Competitive analysis & market research
  • Narrative architecture & story mapping
02
PRE-PRODUCTION · WEEK 2–3

Planning every frame.

Scripts written, storyboards drawn, locations scouted, crews booked. By the time we hit record, every shot is mapped — so production day stays on schedule and on budget.

  • Concept & treatment development
  • Scriptwriting & storyboarding
  • Location scouting & permits
  • Cast, crew & equipment planning
  • Production schedule & shot list
03
PRODUCTION · ON-SET

Capturing the story.

On-set days run by seasoned directors and DOPs, supported by full crew and pro-grade equipment. We capture coverage, B-roll and contingency so post has everything it needs.

  • Direction & cinematography
  • Multi-camera setups & aerial / drone
  • Lighting, grip & sound recording
  • On-set art direction & styling
  • Talent direction & interviews
04
POST-PRODUCTION · DELIVERY

Crafting the final cut.

Editing, colour, sound design, motion graphics — all in-house. Two review rounds, refined to your notes, then delivered in every format your platforms need.

  • Editing & rough cut
  • Colour grading & VFX
  • Sound design, mixing & original music
  • Motion graphics & typography
  • Multi-format export & delivery
